About Us

TGC Animation & Multimedia, Jaipur (Rajasthan) is a premier institute offering top class training in the fields of animation, multimedia, and IT. TGC combines practical, hands-on training with the best knowledge base to equip students for global competitiveness. With a strong emphasis on industry-relevant curriculum, TGC offers courses across graphic design, web development, digital marketing, animation, and more. Expert instructors, state-of-the-art facilities, and a commitment to student success set TGC apart, ensuring passed out students not only excel in their chosen fields but also embody the innovation and creativity needed to lead in the digital age. TGC has branches across jaipur, Delhi NCR and other parts of India.

FAQs: Here’s everything you may ask...

What courses do TGC Jaipur offer?

We offer a wide range of courses in graphic design, photography, web development, video editing, VFx, Gaming, digital marketing, animation, UI/UX design, video editing, Python programming, data analytics, and data science.

Are TGC courses suitable for 10+2?

Yes, our courses are designed to accommodate students of all skill levels, including beginners. We provide foundational knowledge and progressively advance to more complex concepts.

What is the duration of your courses?

Course durations vary, typically ranging from a few weeks for short courses to 6-12 months for comprehensive programs, we also offer 3 years degree course program as well in collaboration with top Indian universities. Speak with our counselor team for more detailed about courses and duration.

Do I need to bring my own laptop/software?

It's recommended to have your own laptop to practice outside of class hours. Although we also provide a system specially for students who can't afford laptops. For software, we provide access to necessary tools and software during the course within our labs.

How do I enroll in a course?

Enrollment can be completed online through our website or by visiting our campus. You'll need to fill out an application form and submit any required documents.

What are the class schedules?

We offer flexible class schedules, including weekday and weekend options, to accommodate the diverse needs of our students. Exact schedules are provided upon course registration.

Do you offer online classes?

Yes, we offer both in-person and online learning options for most of our courses, giving students the flexibility to choose based on their preferences and circumstances.

Is there any financial aid or scholarship available?

We offer various financial aid options and scholarships for eligible students. Details and eligibility criteria can be found on our website or by contacting our admissions office.

Will I receive a certificate upon completion?

Upon successfully completing your course, you will receive a certificate of completion, demonstrating your proficiency and skills in the respective field.

Is there job placement assistance after completing a course?

Yes, we offer job placement assistance, including portfolio reviews, interview preparation, and connections with potential employers to help our graduates secure positions in their chosen fields.

Where is the institute located?

TGC Animation and Multimedia, Jaipur is located near the prime location of World Trade Centre and Gaurav Tower, Location is new commercial hub of Jaipur city with best of the malls and branded flagship stores.
